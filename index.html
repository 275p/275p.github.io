DOBC-Controller

The Disturbance Observer-Based Control (DOBC) controller I designed estimates and compensates for external disturbances affecting the UAV by using an observer to reconstruct the disturbances and adjusting the control input accordingly. This enhances the UAV's robustness, allowing it to accurately track the desired position despite uncertainties or external forces.
